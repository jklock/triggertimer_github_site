Features

Drill Library & Templates

  • 55+ built-in drills across draw, reload, transition, speed, accuracy, and competition work
  • Custom drills with PAR times, shot counts, tags, and full instructions
  • Templates for go-to sessions; quick-launch any drill or template in seconds

Session Engine

  • Per-shot timestamps, cadence, fatigue curve, and round counts captured automatically
  • Run with SG Timer (auto shots), Watch motion/haptics, or manual taps when you need to stay quiet
  • Voice notes and text notes inline with each drill; Live Activity and Siri shortcuts keep the session visible

Analytics & Dashboards

  • Split trends and cadence variance to see how your draw and transitions are moving
  • Drill comparisons with tags, categories, and personal records surfaced automatically
  • Per-drill timelines and session overviews tuned for both quick checks and deep dives

Import / Export

  • Export sessions to CSV or JSON for coaches, review, or backup
  • Import match and practice data (PractiScore and CSV) into your history
  • Share a session package so teammates can load the same drills and targets

Devices

  • SG Timer: BLE pairing, PAR push/pull, live shot streaming, and offline failover
  • Apple Watch: haptic countdowns, motion start, wrist shortcuts, heart-rate tracking for stress insight
  • iPhone & iPad: identical features; iPad adds bigger dashboards, side-by-side compare, and Pencil notes
