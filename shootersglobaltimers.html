Shooter's Global Timers (SG GO)

SG GO and similar shot timers are optional accessories for advanced users who want competition‑grade precision. TriggerTimer works fully on Apple devices alone; adding hardware unlocks pro features and live outputs.

What hardware adds

  • Higher precision: Competition‑grade timing for the smallest intervals.
  • Consistent triggers: Fewer missed or noisy events in loud environments.
  • Live output: Stream timing data to coaches or production setups.
  • Validation & scale: Easier repeatable setups for testing and events.
