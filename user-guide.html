User Guide

TriggerTimer helps you train hands‑free, get instant spoken splits, and review sessions later. Start on iPhone, use Apple Watch for wrist control, or use iPad for deeper analysis.

Quick start

  1. Start a session on your iPhone or Apple Watch.
  2. Use AirPods or phone audio for spoken cues while you train.
  3. Review split times on iPhone or session graphs on iPad.

Where to go next

  • Sessions: Review runs, compare results, and export summaries on iPhone or iPad.
  • Drills: Browse and save templates to keep practice focused and repeatable.
  • Devices: Use Watch or AirPods for hands‑free cues; add hardware timers only if you need pro precision.

Need help?

Contact support@triggertimer.app with device, OS version, and a short description of the issue.
