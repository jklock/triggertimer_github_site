User Guide

Quick start

  1. Create a session from the Sessions tab (tap + or quick-launch a drill/template)
  2. Add drills from the 55+ library or your saved templates
  3. Pick a timing source: SG Timer (auto shots), Watch (motion/haptics), or manual taps
  4. Start the drill, add voice/text notes, and tag it (draw, reload, transitions, etc.)
  5. Review splits, cadence, and fatigue curve; export CSV/JSON if you need to share

Where to go next

  • Sessions & history: Manage active/past sessions, tags, and exports on iPhone and iPad.
  • Drill library: Browse, favorite, and duplicate drills; build templates for repeatable practice.
  • Devices: Pair an SG Timer or Apple Watch for hands-free starts and haptic cues.
  • Analytics: Use iPad dashboards for split trends, category comparisons, and PR tracking.

Troubleshooting

  • SG Timer pairing: Toggle Bluetooth once, open Devices, and re-pair; the app retries and falls back to offline logging if the link drops.
  • Offline training: Manual timing always works; SG and Watch sessions sync back when you reconnect.
  • Exports: CSV/JSON include per-shot timestamps—send to coaches or keep as backup.
  • Need a walkthrough? Ping support@triggertimer.app with device, OS version, and SG model and I’ll send steps.
