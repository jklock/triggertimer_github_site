AirPods — Private audio and cueing

Use AirPods to receive start beeps, PAR warnings, and audio cues without disturbing others. TriggerTimer detects connected AirPods and can route sounds to them selectively while keeping session data on your device.

How AirPods improve training

  • Private start beeps and PAR warnings for focused practice
  • Reduced environmental noise interference when using Watch/phone detection
  • Pair once; TriggerTimer remembers your preference per device

Setup

  1. Pair AirPods with your iPhone/iPad in system Settings
  2. Open TriggerTimer; ensure audio routing preferences are set in Settings → Audio
  3. Choose whether beeps should play to AirPods, device speaker, or both
