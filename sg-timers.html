Shooter's Global Shot Timer Integration

Trigger Timer seamlessly integrates with Shooter's Global Shot Timers, bringing professional-grade timing capabilities to your dry fire training sessions.

Features

  • Wireless Connectivity: Connect your SG timer via Bluetooth for seamless data transfer
  • Real-time Data Sync: Automatically capture and record your shot times
  • Professional Accuracy: Leverage the precision timing of SG Global Shot Timers
  • Historical Tracking: All your timed sessions are saved for future analysis
  • Performance Analytics: Compare your times across different training sessions

Supported Models

Trigger Timer is compatible with all Shooter's Global Shot Timer models that support Bluetooth connectivity. This includes the latest models with advanced features for competitive shooters.

Getting Started

Simply pair your SG timer with your iOS device through the Trigger Timer app. The app will guide you through the setup process, making it easy to start tracking your training sessions immediately.
