iPhone — Instant, hands‑free intervals

TriggerTimer on iPhone gives you instant, hands‑free interval timing. Hear cues, see live splits, and review session summaries in seconds. Use AirPods for private, on‑ear coaching.

What you get

  • Start fast: One tap starts a session so you stay focused.
  • Live splits: See split and lap times on screen as they happen.
  • Audio cues: Clear spoken cues through phone or AirPods.
  • Quick review: Session summaries let you spot errors and progress immediately.
  • No extra gear required: Works great alone; optional hardware is available for pro needs.
