⌚ Apple Watch — Quick Sessions

Train smarter with Trigger Timer on your Apple Watch. Start sessions, get haptic cues, and view quick stats without pulling out your phone.

Features

  • Quick Stats: View your session statistics at a glance
  • Training Timer: Start and stop training sessions directly from your watch
  • Audio Cues: Receive haptic and audio feedback during training
  • Workout Integration: Sessions are logged as workout activities
  • Complications: Add Trigger Timer widgets to your watch face
  • Standalone Operation: Use the app without your iPhone nearby

Compatibility

Trigger Timer supports Apple Watch Series 4 and later, running watchOS 8.0 or higher. The app is optimized for all Apple Watch sizes and takes full advantage of the latest watchOS features.

Convenience at Your Wrist

Whether you're at the range or practicing at home, your training data is always accessible. The Apple Watch app syncs seamlessly with the iPhone and iPad versions, ensuring your progress is tracked across all your devices.
