⌚ WatchOS — Fast-Start Control Surface

The Watch companion is a fast-start control surface: start and stop drills, receive haptic cues, and see quick stats at a glance. Use a watch face shortcut (Live Activity/complication) to launch a preset drill without touching your phone.

Features

  • Quick Stats: View shot counts, averages, and cadence at a glance
  • Fast Controls: Start/stop drills and kick off presets from your wrist
  • Haptic & Audio Cues: Crisp haptics and optional audio keep you in rhythm
  • Watch Face Shortcut: Launch a preset drill via Live Activity or complication
  • Standalone & Sync: Work independently or sync sessions to your iPhone/iPad

Compatibility

Trigger Timer supports Apple Watch Series 4 and later, running watchOS 8.0 or higher. The app is optimized for all Apple Watch sizes and takes full advantage of the latest watchOS features.

Convenience at Your Wrist

Whether you're at the range or practicing at home, your training data is always accessible. The Apple Watch app syncs seamlessly with the iPhone and iPad versions, ensuring your progress is tracked across all your devices.
