The iPhone app gives you fast access to session controls, immediate feedback, and concise stats so you can focus on training.

Features

  • Intuitive Interface: Clean, easy-to-use design optimized for iPhone
  • Detailed Analytics: Comprehensive charts and graphs of your performance
  • Training History: Access all your past sessions and track improvement over time
  • AirPods Integration: Use your AirPods for audio cues during training
  • Session Notes: Add notes to your training sessions for future reference
  • Cloud Sync: Your data syncs across all your Apple devices via iCloud

System Requirements

Trigger Timer requires iOS 15.0 or later and is compatible with iPhone 8 and newer models. The app is optimized for all iPhone screen sizes, from the compact iPhone SE to the largest iPhone Pro Max.

Perfect for Training on the Go

Whether you're at home, at the range, or traveling, your iPhone keeps all your training data at your fingertips. The app works offline, so you can train anywhere without an internet connection.
