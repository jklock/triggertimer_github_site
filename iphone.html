Trigger Timer requires iOS 15.0 or later and is compatible with iPhone 8 and newer models. The app is optimized for all iPhone screen sizes, from the compact iPhone SE to the largest iPhone Pro Max.

Whether you're at home, at the range, or traveling, your iPhone keeps all your training data at your fingertips. The app works offline, so you can train anywhere without an internet connection.
