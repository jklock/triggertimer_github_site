Apple Watch — Hands‑free wrist control

Apple Watch keeps you moving. Start, mark, and stop from your wrist with haptic and visual feedback—no phone needed during sets. Combine with AirPods for discreet audio cues.

What you get

  • Wrist controls: Start and mark intervals on the watch.
  • Haptic feedback: Taps confirm marks without looking.
  • Quick summaries: Brief results shown after each set.
  • AirPods support: Private audio cues routed to earbuds.
  • Purely hands‑free: Ideal for movement‑heavy drills.
