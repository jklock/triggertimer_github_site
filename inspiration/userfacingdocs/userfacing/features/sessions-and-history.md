# Sessions and History Guide

Track, review, and manage your training history.

---

## Overview

The Sessions tab is your training diary. Every session you complete is saved here for review and analysis.

---

## Viewing Sessions

### Sessions Tab

1. Tap the **Sessions** tab
2. See a chronological list of all sessions
3. Most recent sessions appear first

### Session Information

Each session card shows:

- **Title** — Session name or "Quick Session"
- **Date** — When the session occurred
- **Duration** — Total training time
- **Drill Count** — Number of drills completed
- **Rep Count** — Total repetitions recorded

---

## Filtering Sessions

### By Time Period

Use the filter chips at the top:

- **All** — Show everything
- **Today** — Today's sessions only
- **This Week** — Current week
- **This Month** — Current month
- **This Year** — Current year

### By Drill Type

Filter by drill category:

- Draw, Reload, Transition, Accuracy, Speed, Competition, Custom

### Search

Type in the search bar to find sessions by name.

---

## Session Details

Tap any session to see:

### Summary

- Total time
- Average split time
- Best split time
- Slowest split time

### Drill Breakdown

- Each drill in the session
- Times per drill
- Results (hit/miss)
- Notes

### Performance Graph

- Visual timeline of shot times
- Trend line showing improvement
- Outlier highlighting

### Watch Data (if available)

- Heart rate during session
- Heart rate at each shot
- Biometric trends

---

## Editing Sessions

### Edit Session Details

1. Open session details
2. Tap the **Edit** button (pencil icon)
3. Modify:
   - Session title
   - Notes
   - Drill results
4. Tap **Save**

### Add Notes

1. Open session
2. Tap **Edit**
3. Add text notes
4. Save changes

---

## Deleting Sessions

### Single Session

1. Open session details
2. Tap the **Delete** button (trash icon)
3. Confirm deletion

### From List

1. Swipe left on a session
2. Tap **Delete**
3. Confirm

**Warning:** Deleted sessions cannot be recovered unless you have iCloud backup.

---

## Session Recovery

If the app closes unexpectedly during a session:

1. Reopen TriggerTimer
2. A prompt appears: "Recover Interrupted Session?"
3. Choose:
   - **Recover** — Restore the session with recorded data
   - **Discard** — Delete the partial session
   - **Cancel** — Decide later

### How It Works

TriggerTimer saves session checkpoints every 10 seconds during active sessions. If interrupted, this data can be recovered.

---

## Syncing Sessions

### iCloud Sync

When enabled:

- Sessions sync across all your devices
- Training on iPhone appears on iPad
- Data is backed up automatically

### Manual Sync

1. Go to **Settings** → **iCloud Sync**
2. Tap **Sync Now**
3. Wait for sync to complete

---

## Analyzing Trends

Use the **Data** tab for deeper analysis:

- Progress over time
- Category performance
- Personal records
- Training frequency

---

## Tips

1. **Name important sessions** — Makes them easier to find
2. **Add notes after training** — Capture insights while fresh
3. **Review weekly** — Look for patterns
4. **Export regularly** — Keep backups of important data
5. **Compare sessions** — Use the Data tab for trends
