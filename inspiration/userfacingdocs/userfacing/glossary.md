# TriggerTimer Glossary

**Last Updated:** December 2025  
**Purpose:** Single source of truth for branded terms, technical vocabulary, and approved terminology.

---

## Core Terms

### TriggerTimer

- **Definition:** The name of this dry-fire training and shot timer application.
- **Capitalization:** Always "TriggerTimer" (one word, camel case).
- **Usage:** App name, product references, branding.
- **Banned alternatives:** Trigger Timer, triggertimer, TT.

### Session

- **Definition:** A complete training session containing one or more drills.
- **Capitalization:** Lowercase unless starting a sentence.
- **Usage:** Sessions tab, session history, session details.
- **Related terms:** Training session, workout.

### Drill

- **Definition:** A specific training exercise with defined parameters (par time, shot count, category).
- **Capitalization:** Lowercase unless starting a sentence.
- **Usage:** Drills tab, drill library, drill builder.
- **Related terms:** Exercise, routine.

### Repetition (Rep)

- **Definition:** A single timed attempt within a drill.
- **Capitalization:** Lowercase; "rep" acceptable as abbreviation.
- **Usage:** Rep count, repetition list, rep time.
- **Related terms:** Attempt, shot, try.

### Template

- **Definition:** A saved drill configuration that can be reused.
- **Capitalization:** Lowercase unless starting a sentence.
- **Usage:** Template library, create from template.
- **Related terms:** Preset, saved drill.

## Detection and Timing Terms

### PAR Time

- **Definition:** The target time limit for completing a drill.
- **Capitalization:** "PAR" in all caps, "time" lowercase.
- **Usage:** PAR time setting, PAR beep.
- **Notes:** Derived from "par" as in golf (expected performance).

### Split Time

- **Definition:** Time between consecutive shots or clicks.
- **Capitalization:** Lowercase.
- **Usage:** Split display, split analysis.
- **Related terms:** Interval, between-shot time.

### Shot Detection

- **Definition:** The system that detects dry-fire trigger clicks via audio or accelerometer.
- **Capitalization:** Title case when referring to the feature.
- **Usage:** Shot Detection settings, detection sensitivity.

### Click Signature

- **Definition:** The unique audio profile of a gun's trigger click, used to improve detection accuracy.
- **Capitalization:** Title case as feature name.
- **Usage:** Click Signature training, trained Click Signature.

### Click Training

- **Definition:** The process of training the Watch to recognize a specific gun's click sound.
- **Capitalization:** Title case as feature name.
- **Usage:** Click Training section in Watch Settings.

### SG Timer / SG GO

- **Definition:** External Bluetooth shot timer hardware from ShotGlass (SG).
- **Capitalization:** "SG" in caps, "Timer" or "GO" in title case.
- **Usage:** SG Timer connection, SG GO device.
- **Banned alternatives:** SGTimer (no space), shotglass timer.

## Hardware and Connectivity Terms

### Apple Watch

- **Definition:** The watchOS companion device for TriggerTimer.
- **Capitalization:** Official Apple product name casing.
- **Usage:** Watch app, Watch detection, Watch sync.

### Bluetooth / BLE

- **Definition:** Bluetooth Low Energy connection protocol for SG Timer.
- **Capitalization:** "Bluetooth" capitalized, "BLE" as acronym.
- **Usage:** Bluetooth settings, BLE connection.

---

## Data and Analytics Terms

### Heart Rate at Shot

- **Definition:** The user's heart rate captured at the moment of shot detection.
- **Capitalization:** Title case as feature name.
- **Usage:** Biometrics display, performance correlation.

### Progression Mode

- **Definition:** The method by which drills advance during a session.
- **Capitalization:** Title case as setting name.
- **Values:** Manual, Auto-Advance, Random, Timed.

---

## Export Terms

### PractiScore

- **Definition:** Third-party competition scoring platform.
- **Capitalization:** "PractiScore" (one word, camel case).
- **Usage:** Export to PractiScore, PractiScore IDPA format.

### IDPA / USPSA

- **Definition:** Shooting competition organizations (International Defensive Pistol Association, United States Practical Shooting Association).
- **Capitalization:** All caps (acronyms).
- **Usage:** IDPA format, USPSA export.

---

## UI Terms

### Quick Session

- **Definition:** A session started without selecting a specific template.
- **Capitalization:** Title case as feature name.
- **Usage:** Start Quick Session button.

### Live Timer

- **Definition:** The active timing view during a drill.
- **Capitalization:** Title case as feature name.
- **Usage:** Live Timer view, live timing display.
